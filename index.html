How to Use Spencer AI Offline Chatbot

Spencer AI offline chatbot is an intelligent chatbot designed to provide quick and helpful responses. You can use it in both Online and Offline modes automatically.

ðŸŒ Online Mode

When your device is connected to the internet, Spencer AI Offline Chatbot will use it to generate responses. This ensures that you get real-time answers with the latest AI-powered capabilities.

  • Type your message in the chat input
  • Press the "Send" button
  • Wait for the AI to generate a response
  • Responses are saved for offline use automatically
  • Spencer AI learns from them and improves its offline capabilities

ðŸš« Offline Mode

If your device is offline, Spencer AI offline chatbot will switch to Offline Mode, where it provides responses based on learned data with the online chatbots and your prior interactions.

  • If you've asked similar questions before, it will respond based on your history
  • Provides instant responses without internet
  • Uses knowledge saved from online sessions
